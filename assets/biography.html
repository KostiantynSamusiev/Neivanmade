Mykhailo Skop

1993 - Born in Lviv, Ukraine

Artist, illustrator, PhD student.
Ukrainian artist, and Ph.D. student. The primary media are easel graphics, mural art, digital art, and multimedia installation. Since February 24, 2022, Mykhailo has created military posters supporting Ukraine and volunteers. He believes in the power of visual signs because they can ignore geographical and language barriers, making their way straight to the human heart.
